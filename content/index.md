- #player : Anything related to the `player` 
- #npc : Anything related to an `npc`

	- #npc/standing : Current relationship with an `npc`

		- #npc/standing/good
		- #npc/standing/neutral
		- #npc/standing/bad
		- #npc/standing/hostile
